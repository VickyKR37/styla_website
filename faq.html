Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about Styla, how it works, and what you get from your analysis.

What is Styla?

Styla is a mobile app that delivers professional-style colour and style analysis — including your colour season, body shape, line, and scale — based on your own measurements and assessment, without an in-person appointment.

Which devices are supported?

Styla is available for Android. An iOS version is planned — check the home page or app store listing for the latest status.

How do I get my results?

After you complete the in-app steps (measurements and colour assessment), your personalised report is generated immediately on your device. There is no waiting list and no separate email delivery step.

How much does it cost?

Styla is a one-time purchase (currently £6.99 for the colour analysis, £19.99 for the style analysis or £24.99 for both). There is no subscription or recurring fee unless we introduce optional add-ons in the future — any change will be shown clearly before you pay.

Is this the same as seeing an Image Consultant in person?

The analysis is built on professional Image Consultancy methodology, but it is delivered digitally. It is designed to give you a comparable structured output at a fraction of the cost of a face-to-face session — not to replace every nuance of an in-room consultation.

Do I need to upload photos?

You do need to upload a photo for the colour analysis. Please see our privacy page for details on how we store and use this data.

Can I get a refund?

You may have a legal right to cancel your purchase within 14 days under UK consumer law. However, because Styla delivers digital content instantly, by completing your purchase and accessing your results, you agree to immediate delivery and your right to cancel is waived. If you experience a technical issue that prevents your results from being delivered, we will either fix the issue or issue a refund. If you believe there is a fault with your results, you can contact us within 14 days and we’ll review your case. Refunds are not provided once results have been successfully delivered and accessed. For any issues, email support@styla.me.

Who do I contact for help?

See the Support page for contact details and troubleshooting. For legal or privacy questions, you can also refer to the Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.
