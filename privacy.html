Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026. Rai Tech Solutions ("we", "us", "our") operates the Styla mobile application ("App") available on Google Play. We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy rights under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. Please read it carefully before using our App.

If you have any questions, contact us at: support@styla.me

1. Who We Are (Data Controller)

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

Rai Tech Solutions
52 Mornington Crescent, Hounslow, Middlesex, United Kingdom, TW5 9SS
Email: support@styla.me

We are not currently required to register with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) but will do so if and when our processing activities require it.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide Directly

  • Name and email address (if you create an account or contact us)
  • Payment information — processed securely by Google Play or PayPal; we do not store card or bank details
  • Profile preferences such as skin tone, undertone, hair colour, and body line, shape and scale details
  • Any correspondence or feedback you send to us

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • App usage data, session length, and features accessed
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • IP address and approximate location (country/region level only)

2.3 Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect precise GPS location, biometric data, or any data from your device's camera or photo library beyond what you explicitly submit for colour analysis purposes.

3. How We Use Your Personal Data

Providing the App (Contract)

  • Processing your colour and style analysis
  • Generating personalised results
  • Facilitating in-app purchases
  • Sending purchase confirmations and results

Improving the App (Legitimate Interests)

  • Analysing usage patterns
  • Fixing bugs and improving features
  • Monitoring performance and stability

Legal Compliance (Legal Obligation)

  • Maintaining transaction records
  • Responding to lawful requests

4. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Google Play / Google LLC — app distribution, payments, crash reporting
  • PayPal Holdings, Inc. — payment processing
  • Supabase, Inc. — secure cloud database hosting
  • Analytics providers (e.g. Expo / Sentry)

Where data is transferred outside the UK/EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or the UK International Data Transfer Agreement.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: while active + 12 months after deletion
  • Transaction records: 7 years (HMRC requirement)
  • Analytics & logs: 90 days rolling

6. Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use browser cookies. However, third-party SDKs may collect device identifiers for performance monitoring. These are not used for advertising tracking.

7. Children's Privacy

Styla is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If this occurs, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object
  • Rights related to automated decision-making

To exercise your rights, contact support@styla.me. We respond within one month.

You may also contact the ICO at ico.org.uk or call 0303 123 1113.

9. Data Security

We use encryption (TLS), secure cloud storage, and access controls to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App means you accept the updated policy.
